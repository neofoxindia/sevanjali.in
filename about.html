Sevanjali Foundation is an NGO registered under Ministry of Corporate Affairs "CIN: U85300RJ2018NPL063332" - " License No : 113520 " with establish, setup or run medical camps, healthcare institution, medical education objective. It is directly benefitting to poor people and their families, through medical camp welfare projects on health in city , remote villages and slums across the country.
